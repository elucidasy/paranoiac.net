About Me

Hi! My name is Seb and this is my 2023-2024 Humanities Core Digital Archive. I am a first year philosophy major at the School of Humanities and I am originally from San Diego, California. In my spare time I like to make music, create digital art, write, and read. I want to use this digital space to make connections and engage creatively with the material we are learning in Humanities Core.
